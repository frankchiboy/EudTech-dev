FOR YEARS TO COME

15
Years

The system can be divided into infrastructure and copper plates alongside FPGA. One of our most important advantages is that the entire engineering infrastructure of the product can work and serve you for as much as 15 years (during regular maintenance). Thus, our system is most effective for long-term capital investments in your business. In case an upgrade to the FPGA cards is needed, you will simply change the copper cooling component. But the infrastructure will remain in serve for many generations of FPGA.

优势

稳定

Liquid cooling allows FPGAs to work in the normal temperature range, so the system will serve you well for a long time to come. That’s why Comino offers a one-year warranty on its devices — under normal conditions of use and service — which is four times more than that of most air-cooled solutions. Notice also that under ‘normal conditions’ we mean the system operating at full power in 24/7 mode.

静音

The fanless system built around plate heat exchangers is not only effective, but almost completely silent.

升级简单

The heat generated can be reused for other purposes, such as space or water heating.

热能再利用

Comino Grando is designed around quick  disconnect couplings and modular approach, providing you easy access to any node in the system and significantly reducing modernisation costs.

易于维护

Low power consumption compared to traditional air cooling. Comino Grando spends less energy on maintaining the system's temperature, effectively reducing your electricity costs.

低功耗

The cooling system allows Comino Grando to operate safely in any climate, at any time of the year.
